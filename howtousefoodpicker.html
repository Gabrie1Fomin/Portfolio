Welcome to the Food Picker Quiz!

Follow these simple steps to find the perfect food for any situation:

  1. Answer the Questions: The quiz will ask you a series of questions about your preferences, such as meal type (lunch, dinner, etc.), cuisine, temperature, and how much time you have to prepare.
  2. Click "Next": After each question, click the "Next" button to move to the next question. You'll see the possible answers based on your previous choices.
  3. Get Your Result: After answering all the questions, the quiz will give you a result showing the perfect food for you based on your choices!
  4. Try Again: If you want to try a different combination, click "Try Again" to start over with fresh questions.
